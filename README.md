# Balaji Tour & Travels Website

Official website for **Balaji Tour & Travels**, a trusted taxi and travel service providing reliable, safe, and affordable transportation solutions.

🌐 Live Website: https://balajitourtravels.co.in/ 

---

## About

Balaji Tour & Travels offers transportation services for individuals, families, businesses, and events. The platform enables customers to quickly connect and book rides through phone calls or WhatsApp without requiring a dedicated mobile application.

The website highlights the company's services, customer testimonials, luxury vehicle options, and contact information in a modern and responsive interface.
